ODOT Bi-Weekly Tour
Bi-Weekly Tour (BWT) app is an internal ODOT mobile app for iOS. The app was built to streamline road inspections, which used to use paper forms. The app allows workers to quickly save information on locations with deficiencies, including GPS coordinates, labels, descriptions, and photos. The saved data can be exported in a compact manner that is easy for managers to view.
WaffleTracker
Waffletracker is an Android app I developed that allows Wafflepool users to track their miners' hashrate and payout statistics. Users can also get their Bitcoin address balance, in both Bitcoin and US dollars. The app is also available as a webpage.
ODOT Location Finder
Location Finder is a mobile app designed for ODOT roadway technicians and employees available for iOS and Android. The app uses GPS to locate users and return location information, such as mile markers and offsets, that is useful for ODOT reports.
PandaTracker
Pandatracker is an Android app I developed that allows Pandapool users to track their miners' hashrate, as well as their payout statistics by mining round. Additionally, users can get their dogecoin or pandacoin address balance, in both dogecoin/pandacoin and US dollars. The app is also available as a webpage.
ODOT WZ Traffic Control
WZ Traffic Control is a mobile app version of ODOT's "Guidelines for Traffic Control in Work Zones" pocketbook. Users can search through the book's contents and search for work zone information based on work type for the home screen. This app eliminates the need for costly paper pocketbooks.
Calculate My Paycheck
This app calculates paychecks based on hourly wage and can deduct taxes and retirement based on user settings.
ODOT Snow & Ice
ODOT's Snow & Ice internal report website is a patent pending system that utilizes Excel, VBA, and HTML/CSS/JS to generate report web pages on snow events. I optimized web page performance and redesigned the website to simplify the interface and eliminate the need for .pdf reports.
